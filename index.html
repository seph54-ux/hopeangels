About Us

Hope Angels Home Care Agency LLC is dedicated to providing residential, in-home, and community habilitation services. Our approach is person-centered, ensuring that each individual has the autonomy to make their own decisions while thriving in a supportive environment

MISSION

At Hope Angels Home Care Agency LLC, our mission is to empower adults with intellectual disabilities by providing them with the opportunity to live independently. We are dedicated to ensuring each individual has the autonomy to make their own choices and maintain control over their lives. We achieve this by fostering a living environment that is safe, supportive, and respectful of each person's individual needs, freedom, and decisions.

VISION

Our vision for Hope Angels Home Care Agency LLC is to create a person-centered environment where every individual we serve can achieve their fullest potential. We believe that decisions concerning an individual should be directed by the individual themselves. To that end, we are committed to providing an atmosphere that grants them the freedom to make their own choices and shape their own lives.

Our Services

Residential Habilitation

Providing a secure and safe 24-hour environment for individuals with intellectual disabilities.

In-Home Habilitation

Supporting individuals to develop independent living skills within their own homes.

Community Habilitation

Fostering community integration and appropriate social interactions.

Personal Planning

Offering guidance on personal planning and fostering healthy mindfulness.
